General Health Requirements

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle involves a balanced diet, regular physical activity, and proper sleep. Here are some key requirements:

  • Consume a variety of foods from all food groups.
  • Maintain a balanced intake of macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, fats).
  • Stay hydrated by drinking enough water daily.
  • Engage in at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity each week.
  • Get sufficient sleep (7-9 hours per night).

Nutrients

Nutrients are essential for the body to function properly. Here are the main categories:

  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy. Found in grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Proteins: Build and repair tissues. Found in meat, fish, beans, and nuts.
  • Fats: Support cell growth and energy. Found in oils, butter, and avocados.
  • Vitamins: Support various body functions. Found in fruits, vegetables, and supplements.
  • Minerals: Essential for bone health and other functions. Found in dairy, meat, and greens.
  • Water: Vital for hydration and bodily functions.
